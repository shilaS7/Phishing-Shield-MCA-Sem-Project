## üéØ What Problem Does It Solve?

### The Problem: Fake Websites (Phishing)
- **Bad people create fake websites** that look like real ones (like fake banks, shopping sites, or social media)

- **Gives you a safety score** (like a report card for websites)
- **Explains why it's safe or dangerous**
- **Teaches you how to stay safe online**
